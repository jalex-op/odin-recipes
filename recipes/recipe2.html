Chopper's Cotton Candy Salad

Description

Chopper's favorite. Here's what you need to make it.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of spun cotton candy (various flavors)
  • 1 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup of nuts (almonds or walnuts)
  • A drizzle of honey or maple syrup

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, gently mix the cotton candy and mixed berries.
  2. Top with nuts.
  3. Just before serving, drizzle with honey or maple syrup for added sweetness.
