Sky Island Floating Pudding

Description

Wyper's favorite. Here's what you need to make it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of blue jelly (to represent the blue sea)
  • 2 cups of cloud-like whipped cream or meringue
  • 1/2 cup of golden caramel sauce (to represent the golden bell)
  • Edible silver or gold stars for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare the blue jelly as per the packet's instructions and let it set in individual dessert glasses.
  2. Once the jelly is set, layer a thick cloud of whipped cream or meringue on top.
  3. Drizzle the golden caramel sauce over the cloud layer.
  4. Garnish with edible stars.
